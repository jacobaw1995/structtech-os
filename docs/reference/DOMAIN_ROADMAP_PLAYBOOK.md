## q1 — LEAD RESPONSE

**Title:** Instant Lead Response System
**Why:** Speed wins jobs. The contractor who answers first gets the walkthrough — this system makes sure that's always you, even when you're on a job.

- [ASSESS — StructTech] Map how leads reach you today — calls, texts, website, referrals — and where they stall or die
- [BUILD — StructTech] Custom lead capture + instant-response system: every call and form fill gets an immediate text-back and lands in one pipeline
- [ADOPT — Client] Every lead gets a personal follow-up within the response window — no lead sits overnight
- [ADOPT — Client] Log the outcome of every lead conversation in the pipeline — no tracking in your head
- [PROVE — Client] Two straight weeks: every new lead answered in under 5 minutes
- [HANDOFF — StructTech] Team trained on the pipeline, response process documented, runs without you touching it
